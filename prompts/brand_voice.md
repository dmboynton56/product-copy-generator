# Brand Voice Guide

Write with a confident, minimal, editorial tone.

The copy should feel precise and considered, with attention to material, cut, texture, and use. Favor concrete product details over broad claims. Use short, clean sentences and avoid inflated language.

## Style Rules

- Lead with the product's shape, material, or design detail.
- Keep descriptions polished but grounded.
- Use sensory language sparingly and only when it is specific.
- Avoid exclamation points, emojis, slang, and hard-sell phrasing.
- Do not invent sustainability, origin, fit, sizing, or performance claims that are not in the source data.
- Do not use cliches such as "timeless", "effortless", "must-have", "chic", "elevated", "iconic", "versatile", or "luxurious".

## Output Expectations

- Product description: 45-80 words.
- SEO title: under 60 characters.
- SEO meta description: under 155 characters.
- Image alt text: factual and specific, under 125 characters.
